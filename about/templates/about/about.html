Who Are We?

Movie Night is a brand new website that wants to help you sort out your plans for the evening. We prioritise quality over quantity and our goal is to bring you the very best of movies, tv shows and documentaries.

While we understand that physical media is past its glory days, Movie Night acknowledges its importance in the current entertainment landscape and, most importantly, on the preservation of media in the digital age.

Recently, the streaming giants have been cutting content they have exclusivity rights for from their platforms. In an era where an increasing amount of content is being directly sold to those big companies, it's not farfetched to imagine that a portion of that content will irreversibly dissapear for the purpose of cutting costs. Owning physical media is the best day you have to preserve the incredible work of the artists that make your favourite entertainment come to life.

What Can You Expect From Us?

As mentioned above, quality is our focus. We have carefully selected a number of movies, tv shows and documentaries that we believe are at the very top of their category. As time goes by, our database will increase as we add more top tier entertainment options for you to choose from.

We would love to hear from you. Have you been looking for a movie that you've been craving to watch but can't find? Is there a tv show you missed and are eager to bingewatch? Then, tell us about it in our Make a Suggestion form and we will check if we can find it for you.
